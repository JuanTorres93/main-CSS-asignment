About Us

Rhythm Dance Studio is a dance school that offers classes in multiple styles for all levels, focusing on creativity, expression, and community through workshops, performances, and classes.

Meet Our Crew

  • Elena Rostova

    Artistic Director & Ballet Head

    Elena brings the discipline, grace, and passion of classical ballet to our studio. Known for her keen eye for technique and her warm, encouraging teaching style, she believes that anyone can discover their inner elegance. When she isn’t at the barre, Elena loves mentoring young dancers preparing for company auditions.

  • Marcus "Matrix" Brooks

    Lead Urban & Hip-Hop Instructor

    If you want high energy and infectious rhythms, Marcus’s class is where you need to be. He breaks down complex street styles into fun, accessible steps, ensuring every student leaves feeling confident and energized. Marcus focuses on helping dancers find their own unique freestyle voice and stage presence.

  • Carlos Mendez

    Ballroom & Latin Instructor

    Carlos believes that dance is the ultimate form of human connection. Specializing in partner dancing, his classes emphasize communication, rhythm, and frame control. Carlos has a gift for making absolute beginners feel completely at ease on the dance floor, transforming complex footwork into intuitive, joyful movement.

  • Sarah Jenkins

    Studio Coordinator & Youth Program Manager

    Sarah is the heart and soul behind the front desk! She manages our youth programs, ensuring our youngest dancers (ages 3–7) have a safe, imaginative, and joyful introduction to movement. From organizing recitals to answering scheduling questions, Sarah keeps the studio running smoothly and greets everyone with a bright smile.
